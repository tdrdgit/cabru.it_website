Our product categories

A complete range for the research lab, diagnostics and quality control.

Antibodies

Monoclonal and polyclonal primary antibodies and secondary antibodies.

Biochemicals

Active compounds, inhibitors, activators, standards, substrates, tracers, dyes, enzymes.

Immunoassay kits

ELISA, EIA, FC and IHC kits; colorimetric and fluorimetric, for antigen and metabolite assays and the study of biological processes.

Proteins

Recombinant and purified proteins.

Nucleic acids

Reagents and kits for extraction, purification, reverse transcription, amplification (qPCR, PCR), modification, sequencing, NGS and electrophoresis.

Electrophoresis & western blot

Reagents and kits for extraction, purification, assay and electrophoresis of 1D and 2D proteins and for western blot.

Microbiology culture media

Classic and chromogenic media, supplements, agar, peptones, extracts, agarose.

Solutions & powders

Ready-to-use solutions and powders for laboratory preparation.

Plasmas

Deficient and control plasmas for the study of haemostasis and coagulation.

Diagnostic products

Kits and reagents for laboratory diagnostics.

About us

A single point of contact between manufacturers and the laboratory.

CABRU s.a.s. selects specialised international manufacturers in the life sciences and distributes their products on the Italian market. One contact for a range spanning molecular biology, immunochemistry, proteomics, microbiology and diagnostics.

  • Broad range: antibodies, kits, proteins, nucleic-acid reagents, culture media.
  • Selected international manufacturers, represented directly in Italy.
  • A single point of contact for orders, technical information and support.

Frequently asked questions

Frequently asked questions

What does CABRU distribute?

CABRU s.a.s. distributes in Italy reagents, kits, antibodies, proteins, culture media and diagnostic products from the international manufacturers it represents, for research, diagnostics and industry.

How do I request a quotation from CABRU?

Simply specify the product or application of interest, by catalogue code or description. CABRU verifies availability with the manufacturers it represents and prepares a quotation, through a single point of contact.

Which manufacturers does CABRU represent?

CABRU represents 20 specialised international manufacturers in the life sciences. The full list is available in the Brands section.

Is CABRU an exclusive distributor of the brands it represents?

CABRU represents and distributes the listed brands in Italy; unless otherwise stated, it does not operate on an exclusive basis.

Are the products for research or diagnostic use?

The offering includes research-use-only (RUO) products and diagnostic products; the intended use of each product is stated in the manufacturer's documentation.

Where is CABRU based?
