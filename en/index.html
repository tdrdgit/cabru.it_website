What we offer

Our product categories

A complete range for the research lab, diagnostics and quality control.

Antibodies

Monoclonal and polyclonal primary antibodies and secondary antibodies.

Biochemicals

Active compounds, inhibitors, activators, standards, substrates, tracers, dyes, enzymes.

Immunoassay kits

ELISA, EIA, FC and IHC kits; colorimetric and fluorimetric, for antigen and metabolite assays and the study of biological processes.

Proteins

Recombinant and purified proteins.

Nucleic acids

Microbiology culture media

Solutions & powders

Ready-to-use solutions and powders for laboratory preparation.
