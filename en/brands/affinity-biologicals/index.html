Affinity Biologicals in Italy

About Affinity Biologicals

Affinity Biologicals is a manufacturer specialised in haemostasis, with a range dedicated to the study of coagulation and thrombosis.

What Affinity Biologicals makes

Antibodies, EIA and ELISA kits, deficient plasmas and control plasmas for haemostasis and thrombosis research. Reagents designed to study individual coagulation factors and for assay quality control.

Application areas

Coagulation research, study of haemostasis factors and inhibitors, assay development and control. See the Haemostasis & coagulation page.
