DIA.PRO in Italy

About DIA.PRO

DIA.PRO (Diagnostic Bioprobes) is an Italian manufacturer of immunoenzymatic diagnostic kits for the laboratory.

What DIA.PRO makes

The catalogue comprises diagnostic kits in immunoenzymatic format for the measurement of markers and antigens, intended for laboratory diagnostics.

Application areas

The main areas of use are laboratory diagnostics and screening. See the In vitro diagnostics and Toxicology & forensics pages.

DIA.PRO with CABRU

CABRU represents DIA.PRO in Italy as a single point of contact, providing technical information, availability checks, quotation and order handling through to delivery.
