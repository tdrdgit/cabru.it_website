DIA.PRO in Italy

About DIA.PRO

DIA.PRO (Diagnostic Bioprobes) is an Italian manufacturer of immunoenzymatic diagnostic kits for the laboratory.

What DIA.PRO makes

Diagnostic kits in immunoenzymatic format for measuring markers and antigens. Products intended for laboratory diagnostics.

Application areas

Laboratory diagnostics and screening. See the In vitro diagnostics and Toxicology & forensics pages.
