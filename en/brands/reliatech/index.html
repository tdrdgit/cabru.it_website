ReliaTech in Italy

About ReliaTech

ReliaTech (Receptor Ligand Technologies) is a German manufacturer specialised in recombinant proteins, cytokines and growth factors for research.

What ReliaTech makes

Antibodies, recombinant proteins, cytokines and growth factors. Reagents used in functional assays, cell culture and signalling-pathway studies, with a particular focus on angiogenesis.

Application areas

Immunology and inflammation, angiogenesis, cell biology, receptor research. See the Immunology & inflammation page.
