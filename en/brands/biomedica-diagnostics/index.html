BioMedica Diagnostics in Italy

About BioMedica Diagnostics

BioMedica Diagnostics is a manufacturer active in the cardiovascular and haemostasis field, with reagents and components for coagulation research.

What BioMedica Diagnostics makes

The catalogue comprises antibodies, enzymes, proteins, kits, and deficient and control plasmas for the study of cardiovascular disease and coagulation processes.

Application areas

The main areas of use are cardiovascular research, haemostasis and thrombosis, and assay development and control. See the Haemostasis & coagulation page.
