G Biosciences in Italy

About G Biosciences

G Biosciences is a manufacturer with a broad catalogue, focused on proteomics, molecular biology and general laboratory reagents.

What G Biosciences makes

The catalogue comprises kits and reagents for proteomics — protein extraction, quantification and purification, 1D and 2D electrophoresis, mass-spectrometry reagents — together with lab reagents and powders and molecular-biology products.

Application areas

The main areas of use are proteomics, western blot, mass spectrometry and molecular biology. See the Proteomics and Molecular biology pages and the Western blot and 1D vs 2D electrophoresis guides.
