About DGel Sciences

DGel Sciences is a manufacturer specialised in protein-electrophoresis reagents.

What DGel Sciences makes

Protein electrophoresis markers and acrylamide for gel separation. Products for molecular-weight estimation and gel preparation.

Application areas

Proteomics, protein-expression analysis, western blot. See the 1D vs 2D electrophoresis and western blot guides and the Proteomics page.
