BioVendor in Italy

About BioVendor

BioVendor is a manufacturer based in the Czech Republic (Brno), developing and producing immunoassays, recombinant proteins and antibodies. Its range focuses on biomarkers and biological processes, with applications in research and, depending on the line and product, diagnostics.

What BioVendor manufactures

CABRU supplies the BioVendor lines mapped to the represented categories:

  • ELISA kits & assays — immunoassays for biomarkers, hormones, adipokines and other analytes. → ELISA kits
  • Recombinant proteins — antigens and proteins for research and assay development. → Proteins
  • Antibodies — antibodies for immunoassays and target characterisation. → Antibodies

Applications

Endocrinology and metabolism, cardiology and cardiovascular biomarkers, nephrology, inflammation and immunology, and research into biological processes and disease. → Endocrinology · Immunology & inflammation · Cardiovascular

BioVendor with CABRU in Italy

CABRU represents BioVendor in Italy and administers orders as the single local point of contact: request, availability verification, quotation, order handling and technical support. Frequently asked questions

Who distributes BioVendor in Italy?

CABRU s.a.s. represents BioVendor in Italy and administers requests, quotes and orders as the single local point of contact, with technical support.

Which BioVendor products may be ordered through CABRU?

ELISA kits and immunoassays, recombinant proteins and antibodies. Should an item not appear in the catalogue, CABRU verifies it with the manufacturer.

Does BioVendor offer kits for specific biomarkers?

The range covers many biomarkers across endocrinology, cardiology, nephrology and inflammation. On communication of the analyte of interest, CABRU verifies availability.

Are BioVendor kits intended for research or diagnostics?

This depends on the line and product: some are intended for research, others for diagnostic use. The intended use is stated on the manufacturer's datasheet.

How is a BioVendor quote requested?

The product code or the biomarker of interest may be communicated to CABRU, which verifies availability and issues a quote.

Is CABRU the exclusive distributor of BioVendor?

No. CABRU represents and distributes BioVendor in Italy on a non-exclusive basis.
