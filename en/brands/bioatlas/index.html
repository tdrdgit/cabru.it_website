Bioatlas in Italy

About Bioatlas

Bioatlas is a manufacturer of molecular-biology reagents, focused on nucleic-acid amplification and electrophoresis.

What Bioatlas makes

The catalogue comprises reagents for qPCR, PCR and electrophoresis of nucleic acids, including a non-toxic ethidium-bromide (EtBr) substitute for DNA staining, safer to handle and dispose of.

Application areas

The main areas of use are DNA amplification and detection, gel visualisation and routine molecular biology. See the PCR vs qPCR and Nucleic-acid extraction guides.

Bioatlas with CABRU

CABRU represents Bioatlas in Italy as a single point of contact, providing technical information, availability checks, quotation and order handling through to delivery.

Frequently asked questions

Who distributes Bioatlas in Italy?

CABRU represents and distributes Bioatlas in Italy.

Is the EtBr substitute genuinely safer?

It is a DNA stain formulated as an alternative to ethidium bromide, intended to reduce handling and disposal risks; the manufacturer's guidance nonetheless remains applicable.

Does Bioatlas supply qPCR reagents?

Yes, for qPCR and PCR as well as electrophoresis.
