The manufacturers we represent

How we work with manufacturers

CABRU selects specialised manufacturers and brings their products to the Italian market. Not a simple reseller, but a partner that speaks both the language of the laboratory and that of the supplier, verifies availability and follows the order from enquiry to delivery.

Manufacturers by product area

- Antibodies: ExBio, ReliaTech, IMMBIOMED, Affinity Biologicals, BioMedica Diagnostics, Cayman Chemical, BioVendor, FineTest. - ELISA kits: BioVendor, FineTest, LDN, Affinity Biologicals, DIA.PRO, Cayman Chemical. - Proteins: ReliaTech, Enzyme Research Laboratories, BioVendor, FineTest, SEQENS. - Nucleic acids: A&A Biotechnology, Bioatlas, Rovalab, MAGBIO, SMOBIO. - Electrophoresis & western blot: G Biosciences, SMOBIO. - Biochemicals: Cayman Chemical, G Biosciences. - Microbiology media: Condalab. - Solutions & powders: Candor Bioscience, SEQENS, G Biosciences.

Cannot find the manufacturer or product required?

A simple enquiry to CABRU is sufficient: availability is checked with the manufacturers represented and the customer is supported from enquiry to delivery, through a single point of contact.
