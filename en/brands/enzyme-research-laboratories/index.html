Enzyme Research Laboratories in Italy

About Enzyme Research Laboratories

Enzyme Research Laboratories is a manufacturer specialised in coagulation proteins and enzymes, with long experience in the purification of haemostasis factors.

What Enzyme Research Laboratories makes

The catalogue comprises purified coagulation proteins, enzymes, inhibitors and cofactors, intended for the study of individual components of the coagulation cascade and for assay development.

Application areas

The main areas of use are haemostasis and thrombosis research, the study of coagulation factors and assay development. See the Haemostasis & coagulation page.
