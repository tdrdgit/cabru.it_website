Enzyme Research Laboratories in Italy

About Enzyme Research Laboratories

Enzyme Research Laboratories is a manufacturer specialised in coagulation proteins and enzymes, with long experience in purifying haemostasis factors.

What Enzyme Research Laboratories makes

Purified coagulation proteins, enzymes, inhibitors and cofactors. Reagents for studying individual components of the coagulation cascade and for assay development.

Application areas

Haemostasis and thrombosis research, coagulation-factor studies, assay development. See the Haemostasis & coagulation page.
