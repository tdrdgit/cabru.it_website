Condalab in Italy

About Condalab

Condalab (a brand of the Conda group) is a Spanish manufacturer based in Madrid, specialised for decades in culture media and microbiology ingredients. It produces dehydrated media, ready-to-use media, agar, peptones and extracts in-house, with a range serving both research laboratories and industrial quality control.

What Condalab manufactures

CABRU supplies the Condalab lines mapped to the represented categories:

  • Microbiology culture media — classic and chromogenic media, agar, peptones, extracts and sugars for microbial isolation and identification. → Microbiology media
  • Molecular-biology media and agarose for nucleic-acid electrophoresis. → Nucleic acids
  • Base ingredients and powders for media preparation. → Solutions & powders

Applications

Clinical and environmental microbiology, quality control of food, water and pharmaceuticals, and basic microbiology research. → Microbiology

Frequently asked questions

Who distributes Condalab in Italy?

CABRU s.a.s. represents Condalab in Italy and administers requests, quotes and orders as the single local point of contact.

Which Condalab media may be ordered through CABRU?

Classic and chromogenic media, agar, peptones, extracts, sugars, molecular-biology media and agarose. Should a medium not appear in the catalogue, CABRU verifies it with the manufacturer.

What distinguishes classic media from chromogenic media?

Chromogenic media contain substrates that develop colour in the presence of specific microbial enzymes, enabling faster identification than classic media.

Does Condalab also manufacture ingredients for preparing media?

Yes: peptones, extracts, agar and other base ingredients, alongside ready-to-use and dehydrated media.

How is a Condalab quote requested?

The medium reference or the microbiology application may be communicated to CABRU, which verifies availability and issues a quote.

Is CABRU the exclusive distributor of Condalab?
