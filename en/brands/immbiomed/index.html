About IMMBIOMED

IMMBIOMED is a German manufacturer specialised in antibodies for research and laboratory applications.

What IMMBIOMED makes

The catalogue comprises antibodies for use across various immunochemical techniques, selected for specificity and for use in immunoassays and related analyses.

Application areas

The main areas of use are immunochemistry, immunoassays and research. See the Immunology & inflammation page and the Primary and secondary antibody guide.
