ExBio in Italy

About ExBio

ExBio (EXBIO Praha) is a manufacturer based in the Czech Republic, near Prague, specialising in monoclonal antibodies. The range targets flow cytometry and immunology, with purified antibodies and a wide offer of fluorochrome conjugates for multiparametric analysis of cell populations.

What ExBio makes

CABRU supplies the ExBio lines mapped to our categories:

  • Antibodies — purified monoclonal antibodies and flow-cytometry conjugates (CD markers and surface/intracellular antigens) for immunophenotyping and cell analysis. → Antibodies

Applications

Immunology and immunophenotyping, research on cell populations and lymphocyte subsets, inflammation, experimental haematology. → Immunology & inflammation

ExBio with CABRU in Italy

CABRU represents ExBio in Italy and manages orders as the single local point of contact. For flow-cytometry labs, CABRU helps choose the right clone and conjugate (fluorochrome) for the panel and request datasheets. → Request information and a quote

Frequently asked questions

Who distributes ExBio in Italy?

CABRU s.a.s. represents ExBio in Italy and manages requests, quotes and orders as the single local point of contact.

Which ExBio antibodies can I order through CABRU?

Purified and conjugated monoclonal antibodies for flow cytometry (CD markers and other antigens). If a clone or conjugate is not listed, CABRU checks it with the manufacturer.

Does ExBio have conjugated antibodies for multicolour flow cytometry?

Yes: the range includes conjugates with various fluorochromes for multiparametric analysis. Give CABRU the target and fluorochrome and we check availability.

Are ExBio antibodies for research or diagnostics?

It depends on the line and product. The intended use is stated on the manufacturer's datasheet.

How do I request an ExBio quote?

Send CABRU the product code, or the clone/target and fluorochrome of interest.

Is CABRU the exclusive distributor of ExBio?

No. CABRU represents and distributes ExBio in Italy on a non-exclusive basis.
