ExBio in Italy

About ExBio

ExBio (EXBIO Praha) is a manufacturer based in the Czech Republic, near Prague, specialising in monoclonal antibodies. The range targets flow cytometry and immunology, with purified antibodies and a wide offer of fluorochrome conjugates for multiparametric analysis of cell populations.

What ExBio manufactures

CABRU supplies the ExBio lines mapped to the represented categories:

  • Antibodies — purified monoclonal antibodies and flow-cytometry conjugates (CD markers and surface/intracellular antigens) for immunophenotyping and cell analysis. → Antibodies

Applications

Immunology and immunophenotyping, research on cell populations and lymphocyte subsets, inflammation, experimental haematology. → Immunology & inflammation

ExBio with CABRU in Italy

CABRU represents ExBio in Italy and administers orders as the single local point of contact. For flow-cytometry laboratories, CABRU assists in selecting the appropriate clone and conjugate (fluorochrome) for the panel and in requesting datasheets. → Request information and a quote

Frequently asked questions

Who distributes ExBio in Italy?

CABRU s.a.s. represents ExBio in Italy and administers requests, quotes and orders as the single local point of contact.

Which ExBio antibodies may be ordered through CABRU?

Purified and conjugated monoclonal antibodies for flow cytometry (CD markers and other antigens). Should a clone or conjugate not appear in the catalogue, CABRU verifies it with the manufacturer.

Does ExBio offer conjugated antibodies for multicolour flow cytometry?

Yes: the range includes conjugates with various fluorochromes for multiparametric analysis. On communication of the target and fluorochrome, CABRU verifies availability.

Are ExBio antibodies intended for research or diagnostics?

This depends on the line and product. The intended use is stated on the manufacturer's datasheet.

How is an ExBio quote requested?

The product code, or the clone/target and fluorochrome of interest, may be communicated to CABRU.

Is CABRU the exclusive distributor of ExBio?

No. CABRU represents and distributes ExBio in Italy on a non-exclusive basis.
