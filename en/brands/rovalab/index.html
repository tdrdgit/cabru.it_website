Rovalab in Italy

About Rovalab

Rovalab is a German manufacturer of molecular-biology reagents, focused on nucleic-acid amplification.

What Rovalab makes

The catalogue comprises nucleotides, Taq DNA polymerase and PCR mixes for DNA amplification, from single reactions to routine workflows.

Application areas

The main areas of use are PCR and DNA amplification, genotyping and routine molecular biology. See the Molecular biology page and the PCR vs qPCR guide.
