MAGBIO in Italy

About MAGBIO

MAGBIO (MagBio Genomics) is a manufacturer specialised in magnetic-bead technologies for nucleic-acid purification.

What MAGBIO makes

Magnetic beads for extraction and purification of DNA, RNA and PCR products for NGS and sequencing. Reagents designed for sample clean-up and size selection, compatible with automated protocols.

Application areas

NGS library preparation, reaction clean-up, size selection, high-throughput molecular biology. See the Molecular biology page and the Nucleic-acid extraction guide.
