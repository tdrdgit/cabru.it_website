LDN (Labor Diagnostika Nord) in Italy

About LDN

LDN — Labor Diagnostika Nord is a German manufacturer specialised in immunoenzymatic kits, with a particular focus on endocrinology and neuroscience.

What LDN makes

ELISA kits for measuring hormones, metabolites and markers related to endocrinology and neuroscience, including catecholamines, metabolites and steroid hormones.

Application areas

Endocrinology, neuroscience and biomarker research. See the Endocrinology and Neuroscience pages and the ELISA test guide.
