FineTest in Italy

About FineTest

FineTest is the brand of Wuhan Fine Biotech, a manufacturer based in China (Wuhan) specialising in immunoassays and research reagents. It is known for a very broad ELISA catalogue covering many species and targets, complemented by antibodies and recombinant proteins. The range is intended for research.

What FineTest makes

CABRU supplies the FineTest lines mapped to our categories:

  • ELISA kits & assays — broad target coverage across species (human, mouse, rat and more), colorimetric immunoassays. → ELISA kits
  • Antibodies — primary and secondary antibodies for immunoassays and protein analysis. → Antibodies
  • Recombinant proteins for research and assay development. → Proteins

Applications

Immunology and inflammation, oncology, endocrinology and metabolism, and basic research on biomarkers and signalling pathways. → Immunology & inflammation · Oncology
