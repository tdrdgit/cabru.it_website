Candor Bioscience in Italy

About Candor Bioscience

Candor Bioscience is a manufacturer specialised in solutions and buffers that optimise antibody performance in immunoassays.

What Candor Bioscience makes

The catalogue comprises solutions, buffers and stabilisers to improve antibody performance in ELISA, western blot (WB), immunofluorescence (IF), immunocytochemistry (ICC), immunohistochemistry (IHC), immunoprecipitation (IP) and array: blocking agents, diluents, storage stabilisers and background reducers.

Application areas

The main areas of use are immunoassay development and optimisation, the reduction of background and non-specific signal, the improvement of sensitivity and reproducibility, and antibody and conjugate stabilisation. See the Immunology & inflammation page.
