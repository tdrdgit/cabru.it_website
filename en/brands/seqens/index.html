SEQENS in Italy

About SEQENS

SEQENS is an international group active in specialty chemistry and life-science ingredients, including the production of bovine serum albumin (BSA).

What SEQENS makes

Bovine Serum Albumin (BSA) for laboratory use: blocking agent, stabiliser and reference protein in immunoassays, cell culture and preparations.

Application areas

Blocking and stabilisation in ELISA and western blot, cell culture, preparation of solutions and standards. See the Immunology & inflammation page and the Proteins category.

SEQENS with CABRU

CABRU represents SEQENS in Italy as a single point of contact for BSA: technical information, availability, quotation and order handling through to delivery.
