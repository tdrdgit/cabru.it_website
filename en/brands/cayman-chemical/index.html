Cayman Chemical in Italy

About Cayman Chemical

Cayman Chemical is a US manufacturer of life-science research reagents, based in Ann Arbor (Michigan) and active since 1980. It develops and produces small biochemical molecules, immunoassays and high-purity reagents in-house, with a recognised specialisation in lipid and eicosanoid chemistry. Cayman products are intended for research (Research Use Only).

What Cayman Chemical manufactures

CABRU supplies the principal Cayman catalogue lines in Italy, mapped to the represented product categories:

  • Biochemicals — enzyme inhibitors, enzymes, substrates, dyes, tracers, analytical standards and reference compounds. → Biochemicals
  • ELISA kits & assays — assays for antigens, metabolites, lipid mediators and oxidative-stress markers. → ELISA kits
  • Antibodies — primary and secondary antibodies for immunoassays and cell analysis. → Antibodies
  • Recombinant proteins and ready-to-use solutions. → Proteins
  • Eicosanoids & lipids — prostaglandins, leukotrienes, endocannabinoids and other lipid mediators, Cayman's historical field. → Metabolism & lipids

Research areas covered

Oncology, neuroscience, inflammation and immunology, metabolism and lipid biology, oxidative stress, epigenetics, together with toxicology and forensic science (reference standards and compounds for analytical assays).

Frequently asked questions

Who distributes Cayman Chemical in Italy?

CABRU s.a.s. represents Cayman Chemical in Italy and administers requests, quotes and orders as the single local point of contact, with technical support in Italian and English.

How are Cayman Chemical products purchased in Italy?

Purchases proceed by submitting the catalogue number or product description to CABRU via the contact form or email. CABRU verifies availability with the manufacturer, issues a quote and follows the order through to delivery.

Which Cayman Chemical lines may be ordered through CABRU?

Biochemicals and inhibitors, ELISA kits and assays, antibodies, recombinant proteins and eicosanoids/lipid mediators. Should an item not appear in the catalogue, CABRU verifies it with Cayman.

Is Cayman Chemical intended for research or diagnostics?

Cayman reagents are generally intended for research (Research Use Only). For the intended use of a specific product, please refer to the manufacturer's datasheet.

What are eicosanoids and why is Cayman a reference?

Eicosanoids are lipid mediators (prostaglandins, leukotrienes and related molecules) involved in inflammation and cell signalling. Cayman has long specialised in their synthesis and related assays.

Is CABRU the exclusive distributor of Cayman Chemical?

No. CABRU represents and distributes Cayman Chemical in Italy on a non-exclusive basis.
