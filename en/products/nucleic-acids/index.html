Nucleic-acid reagents and kits

What this category covers

The category brings together kits and reagents for the extraction and purification of gDNA, plasmids, RNA and microRNA; reverse transcription; amplification (qPCR and PCR) with Taq polymerase, nucleotides and master mixes; magnetic beads for NGS and sequencing; and nucleic-acid electrophoresis reagents, including non-toxic alternatives to ethidium bromide (EtBr).

Typical applications

Areas of use include molecular biology, genotyping, gene-expression analysis, NGS library preparation and molecular diagnostics. For guidance on the available methods, please refer to the PCR vs qPCR and Nucleic-acid extraction guides.

Who makes them

CABRU represents reagents from A&A Biotechnology (extraction and purification, RT, qPCR/PCR), Bioatlas (qPCR, PCR, electrophoresis, non-toxic EtBr substitute), Rovalab (nucleotides, Taq, PCR mixes), MAGBIO (magnetic beads for NGS) and SMOBIO (DNA markers).

How to request a quote

To obtain information or a quotation, it is sufficient to indicate the starting matrix, throughput and downstream application. CABRU identifies the kits and reagents among the manufacturers it represents and supports the customer throughout the process, via a single point of contact.

Frequently asked questions

Is magnetic-bead or column extraction preferable?

Magnetic beads suit automation and high throughput (e.g. MAGBIO for NGS); columns remain practical for small volumes. The comparison is examined in the extraction guide.

Is there a non-toxic alternative to ethidium bromide?

Yes, among Bioatlas electrophoresis reagents. Details are given on the Bioatlas page.

Are DNA molecular-weight markers available?

Yes, through SMOBIO. Details are given on the SMOBIO page.
