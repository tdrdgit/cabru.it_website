Solutions and powders for the laboratory

What this category covers

Ready-to-use solutions and powders for lab preparation: buffers and stabilisers for immunoassays, blocking agents, bovine serum albumin (BSA) and support reagents for ELISA, western blot and other methods.

Typical applications

Optimising and stabilising ELISA and western blot, reducing background and false positives, preserving antibodies and conjugates, buffer preparation. For the role of BSA and blocking agents, see Immunology & inflammation.

Who makes them

Products from the manufacturers CABRU represents: Candor Bioscience (buffers and stabilisers to optimise antibodies in ELISA, WB, IF, ICC, IHC, IP and array), SEQENS (BSA) and G Biosciences (lab reagents and powders).

How to request a quote

Tell us the method to optimise and the problem to solve (background, stability, sensitivity): we identify the solution among the manufacturers we represent and follow the order to delivery.

Frequently asked questions

Why use a stabilising buffer in immunoassays?

To preserve antibody and conjugate activity and to reduce background and non-specific signal, improving reproducibility and sensitivity. Candor Bioscience specialises in this area.

Do you supply BSA for the lab?

Yes, through SEQENS. See the SEQENS page.

Are the solutions ready-to-use or to be prepared?

Both: ready-to-use solutions and powders for preparation, depending on the reference.
