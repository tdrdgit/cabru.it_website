Solutions and powders for the laboratory

What this category covers

The category comprises ready-to-use solutions and powders for laboratory preparation: buffers and stabilisers for immunoassays, blocking agents, bovine serum albumin (BSA) and support reagents for ELISA, western blot and other methods.

Typical applications

Typical uses include the optimisation and stabilisation of ELISA and western blot, the reduction of background and false positives, the preservation of antibodies and conjugates and buffer preparation. For the role of BSA and blocking agents, please refer to the Immunology & inflammation page.

Who makes them

CABRU represents products from Candor Bioscience (buffers and stabilisers to optimise antibodies in ELISA, WB, IF, ICC, IHC, IP and array), SEQENS (BSA) and G Biosciences (lab reagents and powders).

How to request a quote

To obtain information or a quotation, it is sufficient to indicate the method to be optimised and the issue to be resolved (background, stability, sensitivity). CABRU identifies the solution among the manufacturers it represents and supports the customer through to delivery, via a single point of contact.

Frequently asked questions

Why use a stabilising buffer in immunoassays?

It preserves antibody and conjugate activity and reduces background and non-specific signal, improving reproducibility and sensitivity. Candor Bioscience specialises in this area.

Is BSA available for the laboratory?

Yes, through SEQENS. Details are given on the SEQENS page.

Are the solutions ready-to-use or to be prepared?

Both types: ready-to-use solutions and powders for preparation, depending on the reference.
