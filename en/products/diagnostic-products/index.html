Diagnostic products

What this category covers

Diagnostic kits and reagents intended for the diagnostic field, for the detection and measurement of markers and analytes of clinical interest.

Typical applications

Laboratory diagnostics, screening and enzyme immunoassays in the clinical setting.

Who makes them

Diagnostic products from the manufacturers represented by CABRU: DIA.PRO.

How to request a quote

To check availability and obtain a quotation for a kit it is sufficient to specify the product or marker of interest; CABRU provides the necessary feedback through a single point of contact.
