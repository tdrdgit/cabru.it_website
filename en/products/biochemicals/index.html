Biochemicals for research

What this category covers

The category comprises active compounds, enzyme inhibitors and activators, enzymes, substrates, dyes, tracers and reference standards. These reagents are used to characterise metabolic pathways, modulate enzyme activity and quantify metabolites and small molecules.

Typical applications

Areas of use include pharmacological screening, eicosanoid and lipid research, oxidative-stress and inflammation studies, toxicology and forensic science. The application contexts are illustrated on the Metabolism & lipids and Toxicology & forensics pages.

Who makes them

CABRU represents biochemicals from Cayman Chemical (a broad range of inhibitors, substrates, tracers, standards and eicosanoids) and G Biosciences (proteomics and molecular-biology reagents and standards).

How to request a quote

To obtain information or a quotation, it is sufficient to specify the molecule, purity and format required. CABRU identifies the reference among the manufacturers it represents and supports the customer throughout the process, through a single point of contact.

Frequently asked questions

What analytical standards are available?

Reference standards and tracers for assays and analytical methods, from the manufacturers represented; Cayman Chemical offers a particularly extensive range in this area.

Are these reagents for research or diagnostic use?

Biochemicals are For Research Use Only unless the manufacturer states otherwise.

Can a specific molecule not listed be sourced?

Yes: from the name or CAS number, CABRU verifies availability among the manufacturers it represents.
