ELISA kits and immunoenzymatic assays

What this category covers

The category brings together colorimetric and fluorometric ELISA and EIA kits for the quantitative measurement of antigens, metabolites, hormones and biomarkers. Sandwich, direct, indirect and competitive formats are available for the study of biological processes, in research and diagnostics.

Typical applications

Typical uses include the quantification of serum biomarkers, endocrinology, haemostasis, immunology and toxicology. For an overview of ELISA formats and the standard curve, please refer to the ELISA test guide.

Who makes them

CABRU represents kits from BioVendor, FineTest, LDN (endocrinology and neuroscience), Affinity Biologicals (haemostasis), DIA.PRO (diagnostics) and Cayman Chemical.

How to request a quote

To obtain information or a quotation, it is sufficient to indicate the analyte, matrix and required sensitivity. CABRU identifies the most suitable kit among the manufacturers it represents and supports the customer through to delivery, via a single point of contact.

Frequently asked questions

What ELISA formats exist?

Direct, indirect, sandwich and competitive — the choice depends on analyte and matrix. A full explanation is provided in the ELISA guide.

Are the kits for research or diagnostics?

Both: some manufacturers (e.g. DIA.PRO) offer diagnostic-use kits, others research-use. The intended use is verified case by case.

Are ELISA kits available for hormones and endocrine markers?

Yes, in particular through LDN. Details are given on the LDN page.
