ELISA kits and immunoenzymatic assays

What this category covers

Colorimetric and fluorometric ELISA and EIA kits for the quantitative measurement of antigens, metabolites, hormones and biomarkers. Sandwich, direct, indirect and competitive formats for studying biological processes, in research and diagnostics.

Typical applications

Serum biomarker quantification, endocrinology, haemostasis, immunology and toxicology. To understand ELISA types and the standard curve, see our ELISA test guide.

Who makes them

Kits from the manufacturers CABRU represents: BioVendor, FineTest, LDN (endocrinology and neuroscience), Affinity Biologicals (haemostasis), DIA.PRO (diagnostics) and Cayman Chemical.

How to request a quote

Tell us the analyte, matrix and required sensitivity: we identify the best-suited kit among the manufacturers we represent and follow the order to delivery.

Frequently asked questions

What ELISA formats exist?

Direct, indirect, sandwich and competitive — the choice depends on analyte and matrix. Full explanation in our ELISA guide.

Are the kits for research or diagnostics?

Both: some manufacturers (e.g. DIA.PRO) offer diagnostic-use kits, others research-use — we check case by case.

Do you offer ELISA kits for hormones and endocrine markers?

Yes, notably through LDN. See the LDN page.
