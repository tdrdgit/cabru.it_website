Protein electrophoresis and western blot

What this category covers

Reagents and kits for protein extraction, quantification, purification and electrophoresis (1D and 2D) and for western blot: acrylamide, molecular-weight markers, sample-preparation and transfer reagents.

Typical applications

Proteomics, protein-expression analysis, antibody validation, post-translational-modification studies. For the workflow, see our 1D vs 2D electrophoresis and western blot guides.

Who makes them

Reagents from the manufacturers CABRU represents: DGel Sciences (protein electrophoresis markers, acrylamide), G Biosciences (proteomics: extraction, quantification, purification, 1D/2D electrophoresis) and SMOBIO (protein markers).

How to request a quote

Tell us the technique, molecular-weight range and format: we identify the reference among the manufacturers we represent and follow the order to delivery.

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between 1D and 2D electrophoresis?

1D separates by molecular weight; 2D combines isoelectric point and molecular weight, giving higher resolution for complex mixtures. Details in the dedicated guide.

Do you supply protein molecular-weight markers?

Yes, through DGel Sciences and SMOBIO.

Do you have reagents for the whole western blot workflow?

Yes, notably through G Biosciences for extraction, quantification and separation. See the G Biosciences page.
