Protein electrophoresis and western blot

What this category covers

The category brings together reagents and kits for protein extraction, quantification, purification and electrophoresis (1D and 2D) and for western blot: acrylamide, molecular-weight markers and reagents for sample preparation and transfer.

Typical applications

Typical uses include proteomics, protein-expression analysis, antibody validation and the study of post-translational modifications. For the workflow, please refer to the 1D vs 2D electrophoresis and western blot guides.

Who makes them

CABRU represents reagents from DGel Sciences (protein electrophoresis markers, acrylamide), G Biosciences (proteomics: extraction, quantification, purification, 1D/2D electrophoresis) and SMOBIO (protein markers).

How to request a quote

To obtain information or a quotation, it is sufficient to indicate the technique, molecular-weight range and format. CABRU identifies the reference among the manufacturers it represents and supports the customer through to delivery, via a single point of contact.

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between 1D and 2D electrophoresis?

1D electrophoresis separates by molecular weight; 2D combines isoelectric point and molecular weight, providing higher resolution for complex mixtures. Details are given in the dedicated guide.

Are protein molecular-weight markers available?

Yes, through DGel Sciences and SMOBIO.

Are reagents available for the whole western blot workflow?

Yes, in particular through G Biosciences for extraction, quantification and separation. Details are given on the G Biosciences page.
