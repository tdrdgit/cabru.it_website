Antibodies for research and diagnostics

What this category covers

The category brings together monoclonal and polyclonal primary antibodies, secondary antibodies and conjugates for a wide range of techniques: ELISA, western blot, immunofluorescence (IF), immunocytochemistry (ICC), immunohistochemistry (IHC), immunoprecipitation (IP) and flow cytometry. These reagents are selected for specificity and reproducibility in the detection of antigens, markers and molecular targets.

Typical applications

Antibodies are used in immunoassays, protein-target validation, cell phenotyping by flow cytometry and the study of signalling pathways and biomarkers. The relevant use contexts are described on the Immunology & inflammation and Haemostasis & coagulation pages.

Who makes them

CABRU represents antibodies from several manufacturers: ExBio (flow-cytometry monoclonals), Cayman Chemical, ReliaTech, BioVendor, FineTest, IMMBIOMED, Affinity Biologicals and BioMedica Diagnostics.

How to request a quote

To obtain information or a quotation, it is sufficient to indicate the target, application and format required. CABRU identifies the most suitable reference among the manufacturers it represents and supports the customer throughout the process, from enquiry to delivery, through a single point of contact.

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between a primary and a secondary antibody?

The primary antibody binds the target antigen directly; the secondary binds the primary and carries a detection label (enzyme or fluorophore). Further detail is available in the dedicated guide.

Does CABRU supply antibodies for flow cytometry?

Yes, in particular ExBio's conjugated monoclonals. Details are given on the ExBio page.

Is it possible to request an antibody not listed in the catalogue?

Yes: from the target description, CABRU verifies availability among the manufacturers it represents.
