Microbiology culture media

What this category covers

The category comprises classic and chromogenic culture media, agar, peptones, extracts, sugars, supplements, molecular-biology media and agarose. These products are intended for the isolation, enumeration, identification and culture of microorganisms.

Typical applications

Areas of use include clinical, environmental and food microbiology, industrial quality control and research. To understand the benefits of chromogenic media, please refer to the dedicated guide and the Microbiology page.

Who makes them

CABRU represents media from Condalab, with a broad range of classic and chromogenic media, agar, peptones, extracts, sugars, molecular-biology media and agarose.

How to request a quote

To obtain information or a quotation, it is sufficient to indicate the target organism, method and format (dehydrated or ready-to-use). CABRU identifies the most suitable medium among the manufacturers it represents and supports the customer throughout the process, via a single point of contact.

Frequently asked questions

What are chromogenic media?

They are media that use chromogenic substrates to produce species-specific coloured colonies, simplifying identification. Further detail is available in the dedicated guide.

Are agar, peptones and supplements also available?

Yes, the full range through Condalab. Details are given on the Condalab page.

Are the media for clinical, food or industrial use?

They span several fields; the reference suited to the intended use is identified at the enquiry stage.
