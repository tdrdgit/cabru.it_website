Microbiology culture media

What this category covers

Classic and chromogenic culture media, agar, peptones, extracts, sugars, supplements, molecular-biology media and agarose. Products for the isolation, enumeration, identification and culture of microorganisms.

Typical applications

Clinical, environmental and food microbiology, industrial quality control, research. To understand the benefits of chromogenic media, see the dedicated guide and the Microbiology page.

Who makes them

Media from the manufacturers CABRU represents: Condalab, with a broad range of classic and chromogenic media, agar, peptones, extracts, sugars, molecular-biology media and agarose.

How to request a quote

Tell us the target organism, method and format (dehydrated or ready-to-use): we identify the best-suited medium among the manufacturers we represent and handle the order through one point of contact.

Frequently asked questions

What are chromogenic media?

Media that use chromogenic substrates to produce species-specific coloured colonies, simplifying identification. More in the guide.
