Recombinant and purified proteins

What this category covers

The category comprises recombinant and purified proteins, cytokines, growth factors and native proteins, including enzymes and cofactors. These reagents are intended for molecular-interaction studies, functional assays, cell culture and assay development.

Typical applications

Areas of application include immunology and inflammation research, angiogenesis, cell differentiation, haemostasis and coagulation. The use contexts are described on the Immunology & inflammation and Haemostasis & coagulation pages.

Who makes them

CABRU represents proteins from ReliaTech (cytokines and growth factors), BioVendor, FineTest, Enzyme Research Laboratories (coagulation proteins and enzymes) and SEQENS (BSA).

How to request a quote

To obtain information or a quotation, it is sufficient to indicate the protein, species, expression system and purity grade. CABRU identifies the reference among the manufacturers it represents and supports the customer throughout the process, via a single point of contact.

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between a recombinant and a purified protein?

A recombinant protein is expressed in a heterologous system; a purified protein is isolated from the native source. The choice depends on the required activity and application.

Are cytokines and growth factors available?

Yes, in particular through ReliaTech. Details are given on the ReliaTech page.

Are coagulation proteins available?

Yes, through Enzyme Research Laboratories and BioMedica Diagnostics.
