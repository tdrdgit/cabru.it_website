Recombinant and purified proteins

What this category covers

Recombinant and purified proteins, cytokines, growth factors and native proteins, including enzymes and cofactors. Reagents for molecular-interaction studies, functional assays, cell culture and assay development.

Typical applications

Immunology and inflammation research, angiogenesis, cell differentiation, haemostasis and coagulation. Use contexts in Immunology & inflammation and Haemostasis & coagulation.

Who makes them

Proteins from the manufacturers CABRU represents: ReliaTech (cytokines and growth factors), BioVendor, FineTest, Enzyme Research Laboratories (coagulation proteins and enzymes) and SEQENS (BSA).

How to request a quote

Tell us the protein, species, expression system and purity grade: we identify the reference among the manufacturers we represent and handle the order through one point of contact.

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between a recombinant and a purified protein?

Recombinant is expressed in a heterologous system; purified is isolated from the native source. The choice depends on activity and application.

Do you supply cytokines and growth factors?

Yes, notably through ReliaTech. See the ReliaTech page.

Are coagulation proteins available?

Yes, through Enzyme Research Laboratories and BioMedica Diagnostics.
